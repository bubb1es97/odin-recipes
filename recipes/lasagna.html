Lasagna

Description

Do you want a delectable experience the whole family can enjoy? Try this yummy lasagna recipe. Layer it up in a pan, place in the oven and, BAM! All done!

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • noodles
  • cottage cheese
  • italian seasoning
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Turn oven on bake at 375F
  2. Cook ground beef and add salt
  3. Boil noodles according to package instructions
  4. mix cottage cheese and italian seasoning
  5. lay noodles on bottom of 9x13 pan
  6. add a layer of ground beef
  7. add layer of mozzarella cheese
  8. add layer of cottage cheese and italian seasoning mix
  9. add layer of tomato sauce
  10. repeat layering process till pan is seven eighths full
  11. Bake for an hour
  12. add mozzarella cheese on top and put back in oven for ten minutes

Pull lasagna out of the oven and leave it to set and cool for ten minutes

And that's it! Great job cooking your restaurant worthy lasagna
