Bone Broth

Description

Bone broth is not only great for chasing the cold away when you want to sip on something warm, but it's also been scientifically proven to boost the immune system with it's high collagen content. Try this recipe when you feel a cold coming on or when you are simply craving a hearty and rich beverage.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Marrow bones
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Fresh thyme
  • Yellow onion
  • Three carrots
  • Celery stalk

Steps

  1. Put marrow bones in crock pot
  2. Fill with filtered water
  3. Add three tablespoons of Apple Cider Vinegar
  4. Add salt and Fresh thyme
  5. Chop up and saute on low the onion, carrots and celery stalk
  6. Add the sauted onion, carrots and celery stalk to the crock pot
  7. Cook on low for twenty hours

When twenty hours are up let cool for thirty minutes

You can add to soups for extra flavor or drink straight up. Enjoy and stay warm out there!
